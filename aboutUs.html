-- About Us --

Welcome to Paradise Hotel!

The hotel is a family-run hotel with 10 rooms offering comfortable sleep with a very nice view. Our hotel is located above Sokovia and offers a unique hotel experience for modern travelers. Take in the breathtaking views from our terraces. You can enjoy beautiful sunsets in from Meenachil Hostel.

The hotel is located in the silent part of Sokovia that will ensure have a nice sleep in the evenings. Our goal is for our guests to feel at home. We offer all the facilities that a five star hotel has. While we offer a comfortable stay at a unique location, we highlight the local culture.

We welcome you to our boutique hotel in Sokovia. :)
